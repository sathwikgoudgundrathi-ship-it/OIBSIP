Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Scientist, Teacher, Author and the People's President of India

Why I admire him

Dr. Kalam proved that discipline, humility and curiosity can take a person from a small town to the highest office in the country. His life continues to inspire students to dream boldly, study sincerely and serve society with honesty.

1931Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
1960sStarted his career in aerospace and defense research.
1998Played a major role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
2002Became the 11th President of India.
2015Passed away while doing what he loved most: teaching students.

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
